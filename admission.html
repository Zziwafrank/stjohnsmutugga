Admission Requirements

  • Student must meet the required age for the class level.
  • Previous academic reports must be submitted.
  • Student must pass the entrance interview or test.
  • Parents/guardians must complete the admission form.
  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Reports
  • Passport Photos
  • Medical Form
  • Parent/Guardian Identification

Application Process

Step 1

Collect or download the admission form.

Step 2

Fill in the form and attach required documents.

Step 3

Submit the application to the school office.

Step 4

Attend the admission interview or entrance test.
